  • Start with £100 in your pot.
  • Get exactly 21 to win £25.
  • Your pot is saved, so you can continue playing later.
  • If you draw a 1 or 11 (ace), you can choose which value to use.
  • If your total is less than 21, you can draw another card.
  • If your total goes over 21, you lose £5.
